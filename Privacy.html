Privacy Policy · SanppyRoll

Effective: May 21, 2026 Managed by Haider & Team Worldwide Compliance

Your trust is our priority. This policy explains how SanppyRoll handles your data when you stream, browse, and interact with our platform.

1. Information We Collect

At SanppyRoll (including our web app, mobile views, and related services), we collect limited and necessary information to provide a seamless streaming experience:

  • Account details – email address, username, and hashed authentication data (via Firebase Auth).
  • Watch activity – content history, watch progress (resume points), and list interactions to keep your "Continue Watching" updated.
  • Device & usage data – IP address, browser type, operating system, and anonymized analytics to improve performance.
  • Optional profile info – avatar images you upload or retrieve from providers (Google, etc.).

No sensitive financial data is stored directly on our servers – all payments are processed via secure third-party gateways (if applicable).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively for:

  • Personalizing recommendations – showing trending movies, series and anime that match your taste.
  • Maintaining watch history & progress so you never lose your place across devices.
  • Improving platform stability & UI – fixing bugs, optimizing CDN delivery and reducing buffering.
  • Security & fraud prevention – protecting accounts from unauthorized access.
  • Communicating service updates – we may send essential notifications about policy changes or new features (opt-out available).

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Absolutely no data brokerage.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

To offer a smooth experience (remembering preferences, authentication state, and search history), SanppyRoll uses minimal cookies and local browser storage. You can manage consent via our cookie banner or browser settings.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, routing, and server load balancing.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use aggregated, anonymized data (via Firebase Analytics) to understand traffic patterns — no personal identifiers are shared.
  • Opt-out: You may decline non-essential cookies via the cookie banner, but certain features may be limited.

Third-party services (YouTube trailers, embedded content) may set their own cookies according to their policies.

4. Third-Party Services

We leverage trusted platforms to build SanppyRoll. Each service adheres to strict data protection standards:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, real-time database, storage. Firebase Privacy
  • YouTube API Services: For trailers and embedded content. User interactions with YouTube are subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram: Public community channel links – when you click join, Telegram's own privacy terms apply.
  • CDN & Hosting: Media streaming through secured servers; IP addresses are processed transiently for content delivery.

We ensure that all third parties are GDPR/CCPA compliant where applicable.

5. Data Security & Retention

Protecting your information is non-negotiable. We employ:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) for all communications.
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting unauthorized read/write access.
  • Regular security audits and automated vulnerability scans.

Account data is retained as long as your profile is active. If you request deletion, we anonymize or erase your watch history within 30 days. Inactive accounts may be purged after 24 months of no activity.

In case of any data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours via email.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, etc.), you have rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access – obtain a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification – correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure – request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing – limit how we use your data.
  • Data Portability – receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise any rights, contact us at: privacy@sanppyroll.com or use the in-app "Delete Account" feature. We respond within 30 days.

7. Children’s Privacy

SanppyRoll is intended for users aged 13+ (or minimum age required by your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can delete it immediately. Parental control features are available via device-level settings.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, operational, or feature changes. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates the latest revision. Significant changes will be highlighted via an in-app notification or email. Continued use of SanppyRoll after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
